Top 3 Dramas of all time

My Liberation Notes

Its a korean series narrates about everyday struggle of 3 bachelor siblings.The plot dont have so much twist but every episode shows how beautiful our life is if we decided to live it fully.The end gives us a realization of not everybody out their is having fun and living happily but still we can live the best of it

Mr Queen

The best romantic comedy korean series. Its about a chef who fell into a swimming pool while running away from the bad guys.The chef went into coma and his spirit woke up in josen era's princess who were about to get marry to the king.

Coffee Prince

The first korean romantic drama I ever watched .This drama made me fall in love with the kdramas. Its about a girl who dress up like a boy so she can get many jobs as she can.The cafe owner she works for fell in love with the boy version of herslef even though he is a straight guy.The confusion casuing plot makes this drama extra special.
